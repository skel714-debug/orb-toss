Technology Hub · Orb Toss

Point at an orb (controller or bare-hand ray), pinch or squeeze to pull it to you, throw it through the ring. In Mixed Reality the game maps your real room: orbs bounce off your walls and furniture, and the ring anchors to real walls (requires Space Setup on the headset). Streaks multiply points; gold rings double; final 15s is Frenzy. Kiosk pads: START / EXIT. Left stick: teleport · Right stick: snap turn · A: reset orbs · B: reset scores.
