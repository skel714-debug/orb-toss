Technology Hub · Orb Toss

Point at an orb (controller ray, or bare-hand ray) and squeeze trigger/grip or pinch — even from a distance, it flies to your hand. Release mid-swing to throw it through the ring. Streaks multiply points. Hit the START pad for a 60-second round. Left stick: teleport · Right stick: snap turn · A: reset orbs · B: reset scores. Desktop preview: drag to look.
